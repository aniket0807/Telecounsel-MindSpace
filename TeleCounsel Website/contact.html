Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?
We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our support line.
How do I schedule an appointment with a therapist?
You can schedule appointments through your user dashboard after creating an account. Simply log in, browse available therapists, and select a time that works for you.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, HSA/FSA cards, and PayPal. For information about insurance coverage, please contact our billing department.
How can I become a therapist on MindSpace?
Licensed mental health professionals can apply through our Therapist Portal. We require verification of credentials, professional references, and an interview process.
